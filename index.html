FAQ

Quick answers customers always ask.<️>

Do I need monthly hosting?

No. GitHub Pages can host for free. You only pay for your domain (usually yearly).

Can I edit it myself?

Yes. I can deliver a simple update workflow (Dreamweaver or just editing files + pushing to GitHub).

Can you connect online ordering / POS later?

Yes. Many POS systems support links, embedded menus, or hosted ordering pages we can integrate.

How fast can we launch?

Starter sites can launch in 1–3 days if content is ready.
