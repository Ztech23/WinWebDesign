Can I edit it myself?

Yes. The site is delivered as an open, version-controlled project, allowing approved updates at any time. I include a straightforward update workflow so content changes remain safe and predictable.

Can you connect online ordering / POS later?

Yes. Many POS systems support links, embedded menus, or hosted ordering pages we can integrate.

How fast can we launch?

Starter sites can launch in 3–5 days if content is ready.
